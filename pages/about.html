About

AJ's BBQ and CAFE opened its door in October 2014 with one goal in mind: to share Filipino Culture and Food with our community. This is the place where you can find the most authentic, home cooked Filipino dishes in the Bay Area. Our shop is open Monday - Friday, 6am to 8pm & Saturday 8am to 7pm.
